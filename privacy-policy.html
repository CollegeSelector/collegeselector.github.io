Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 14, 2017

Welcome to the College Selector (“College Selector,” “we,” “us” or “our”). College Selector provides an easy means to record the opinions of the user on the Universities they’re looking at.

Our Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information in relation to our mobile services, web site, and any software provided on or in connection with College Selector services (collectively, the “Service”), and your choices about the collection and use of your information.

By using our Service you understand and agree that we are providing a platform for you to show your thinking in order to fuel the conversations between parents and their children.

Our policy applies to all visitors, users, and others who access the Services (“Users”).

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information

Information you provide us directly:

  • Your password and email address when you register for a College Selector account.
  • Profile information that you provide for your user profile (e.g. SAT and ACT scores, Grade Point Average, major, interests, activities, and goals). This information is placed into the profile page. It’s use is to help you compare the schools and how you feel you fit in and whether you feel it’s a possible option.

    • 2. How we use your information

    In addition to some of the specific uses of information we describe in this Privacy Policy, we may use information that we receive to:

  • Help you efficiently access your information after you sign in
  • Remember information so you will not have to re-enter it during your visit or future visits
  • Showcase your information to help you compare your universities.

    • 3. Sharing of your information

    We will not rent or sell your information to third parties outside College Selector without your consent.

    4. How we store your information

    Storage and Processing:

  • Your information collected through the Service is stored in a database off the device.
  • Your information is encrypted when sent and decrypted when fetched from the database.
  • By registering for and using the Service you consent to the storing of data elsewhere.

    • 5. Your choices about your information

    Your account information and profile / privacy settings:

  • Update your information when necessary for your own good

    • How long we keep your User Content:

  • Following termination or deactivation of your account, College Selector may retain information (including your profile information) and User Content for a working week before it is deleted off the database.

    • 6. Children's Privacy

  • College Selector does not knowingly collect or solicit any information from anyone under the age of 13 or knowingly allow such persons to register for the Service. The Service and its content are not directed at children under the age of 13. In the event that we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under age 13 without parental consent, we will delete that information as quickly as possible. If you believe that we might have any information from or about a child under 13, please email me at: erees.hk@splusgroup.net.
